Matthias Leimeister

Hi, I'm a DSP research engineer at Native Instruments, working in the area of music information retrieval. Before I was at Fraunhofer IDMT as a research student for the project Songs2See, completed an M.Sc. in Media Technology at TU Ilmenau with a focus on audio coding and analysis, and a diploma in mathematics at Ruprecht-Karls-University Heidelberg. Besides music analysis and processing, I'm also interested in speech recognition, computer vision and natural language processing. You can find some of my private projects at @mleimeister.
